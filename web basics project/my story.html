This is my story

From a small town in India to the vibrant heart of New York, my journey has been one of personal discovery and growth. Growing up, I was always drawn to creativity and storytelling, using art and design as ways to express myself.

Moving to New York felt like a dream, brought to life because of a lot of luck, hard work and support from my parents. Each step has shaped who I am, from the classrooms to the real-world projects where I saw the impact my work could make. Along the way, I’ve come to see design not just as a craft but as a way to build connections, support communities, and make meaningful change.
